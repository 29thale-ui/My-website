The Changeup

Keeping the hitters off balance with a slow pitch.

Grip and Mechanics

Use a grip with the thumb on the bottom and two middle fingers together to slow it down.

Velocity

The changeup is known for its slow speed compared to a fastball.

Strategy

Use it when the hitter expects a fastball.

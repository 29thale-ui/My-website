Top 3 most common pitches

The most common pitch is the 4 seam fastball. The 4 seam fast ball is meant to have speed and be accurate

The seconf most common pitch is the Slider. The slider is meant to look like a fastball but then drop at the last second to decieve the batter.

The third most common pitch is teh 2 seam or sinker. The sinker is meant to look like a slow pitch before curving towards the plate to make the batter think its an easy hit before sinking.
